NICE TO MEET YOU,


Hi, I'm Iniyavan Mageshwaran. I'm a self-taught UX/UI Designer. I've been learning on my own for 2 years, and I've had the opportunity to work on some really exciting projects. I'm passionate about UX/UI Designer, and I'm always looking for new ways to learn and grow.

I started learning Designing courses because I was always interested in Designing. I knew that I wanted to learn how to do it, but I didn't have the money or the time to go to institute. So, I decided to teach myself.

I started by reading books and articles about UX/UI. I also watched online tutorials and took online courses. I spent a lot of time practicing and experimenting, and eventually I started to get the hang of it.

After a few years of self-study, I was able to get a job as a Designer from @Nithra Apps. I'm now working on some really exciting projects, and I'm learning new things every day. Now I promoted to Senior Designer in Nithra apps.

I'm excited to share my story with you, and I hope it inspires you to learn something new. If you're interested in learning UX/UI, but you don't know where to start, I encourage you to check out the resources that I mentioned. With a little hard work and dedication, you can learn anything you set your mind to.
