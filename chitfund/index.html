How Does It Work?

It's just 4 simple steps to manage your money with friends.

01

1. Join a Group

Join a group of 10-20 people. Everyone agrees to pay the same amount every month.

02

2. Pay Monthly

Collect money from everyone. Example: 10 people pay ₹1000 = ₹10,000 Total Pot.

03

3. Need Money? Ask!

If you need the full amount, you can "bid" for it. The person who needs it most gets it.

04

4. Everyone Saves

The "discount" from the winner is shared with everyone else. This lowers your next payment!
